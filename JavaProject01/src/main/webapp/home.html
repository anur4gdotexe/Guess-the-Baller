HOW TO PLAY?
  • Each guess must be a valid player name
  • Each player is assigned with following fields:
    1. Player's Club
    2. The league the player is a part of
    3. Player's favoured position
    4. Player's nationality
    5. Player's age group (group of fives)
  • If your guess's field matches with the key player's field, the respective tile turns green; red otherwise.
  • You'll get five chances to Sherlock Holmes our key player
